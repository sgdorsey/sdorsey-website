About

I am a first-year PhD student in the Digital History program at Clemson University. I specialize in southern history, particularly labor relations and religious history as it pertains to textile mill communities in upstate South Carolina. Before coming to Clemson, I received Bachelors Degrees in History, Psychology, and Bible and Religion from Erskine College in 2024. Feel free to explore any of the projects on this site or contact me at sldorse@clemson.edu.
